Our Story

Founded in 2015 by the Mehta family, Serenity Homestay began as a small farmhouse and grew into a boutique eco-stay. We believe travel should heal, not just tour. Every material is locally sourced, every meal is farm-to-table, and every guest is treated like family.

We are proud to be a plastic-free property, run on solar energy, and actively involved in local forest conservation.
